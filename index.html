ASTRAL
Activity Scanner Tool for Risk Assessment of Logs

Find the signal in the noise.

ASTRAL reads a sea of AI chat logs looking for the rare conversation that’s really about causing biological harm — and hands a human reviewer the few worth a closer look. How well it does that comes down to a handful of numbers. This is where you get to feel what they mean, no statistics required.

Everything here is one living world — a crowd of sessions you shape with a few dials. Nudge a dial and every metric moves at once. That’s the whole trick, and it’s kind of fun to watch.
